# Jamezzi Homepage — Locked Design Specification

Status: Approved for implementation  
Brand: Jamezzi, the personal brand and education platform of James Alexandre  
Primary goal: Build James Alexandre's reputation and attract serious opportunities  
Secondary goal: Direct visitors to Jamezzi Academy

## 1. Brand Positioning

Jamezzi is the home of James Alexandre's work and teaching. It brings together his digital products, portfolio, practical guides, online courses, books, and occasional in-person seminars.

The public website serves discovery and credibility. The authenticated Academy is a separate, focused learning environment.

## 2. Public Navigation

The logo is the Home link. Do not include a separate Home item.

Desktop order:

`[Jamezzi]  Portfolio  Guides  About  [Academy]  EN ▾  Sign In`

- Academy is visually distinguished but refined, not an aggressive sales button.
- Language options: English (EN), Français (FR), Kreyòl (HT).
- Use language names, not flags.
- Switching language preserves the current equivalent page when available.
- On mobile, show Jamezzi at left and Academy plus a menu trigger at right.

Authenticated students enter a different navigation environment:

- My Learning
- My Courses
- My Books
- Events
- Certificates
- Help
- Account
- Sign Out

Portfolio, Guides, and About do not appear in the Academy student navigation. `Visit Jamezzi.com` may appear in the profile menu.

## 4. Hero

Eyebrow: `JAMES ALEXANDRE`

Headline:

> Ideas are only valuable when they become real.

Supporting copy:

> I design and build practical digital products—and turn what I know into courses, books, and live training.

Actions:

- Primary: `Explore My Work`
- Secondary: `About James`

### Hero art direction

- One integrated responsive visual environment beginning behind the navigation.
- No separate hero image and no image stacked below the copy on mobile.
- Abstract field moves primarily from upper-right toward lower-left.
- Use indigo, violet, restrained warm coral, translucent planes, construction lines, subtle contours, and rough marks resolving into precise geometry.
- Warm Paper negative space protects readability.
- Same visual concept across mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- Real product interfaces begin in Selected Work, not the hero.
- No laptops, desks, floating dashboard cards, device mockups, or generic gradient blobs.
- The principle may feel immersive like premium technology sites, but artwork must be original to Jamezzi.

## 13. Grid and Spacing

- Maximum container: 1320px.
- Mobile gutter: 20px, increasing to 24px on larger phones.
- Tablet gutter: 32px.
- Desktop gutter: 48px.
- Large desktop gutter: 64px.
- Grid: 4 columns mobile, 8 tablet, 12 desktop.
- Grid gaps: 16px mobile, 24px tablet, 24–32px desktop.
- Reading column: 680–720px; wide editorial text maximum 820px.

Section spacing:

- Mobile: 80–112px depending on narrative importance.
- Tablet: 96–144px.
- Desktop: 120–192px.

Radii:

- Buttons and inputs: 12px.
- Standard cards: 16px.
- Large showcases: 24px.
- Browser/product frames: 18px.
- Pills only for genuinely pill-shaped labels.

Use quiet 1px borders and minimal shadows. Do not outline every section or apply large radii everywhere.

## 14. Mobile Composition

- Hero artwork and text are one integrated first-viewport composition.
- No separate image below hero copy.
- Header contains Jamezzi, Academy, and menu trigger.
- Product screens use focused readable crops rather than shrunken dashboards.
- Nichr, Endize, and clienIQ become substantial full-width product stories.
- Academy learning paths and guide stories stack with intentional hierarchy, not generic equal cards.
- Important controls have at least 44px touch targets.
- No horizontal overflow or essential horizontal carousels.
- Fix layout pressure before reducing text sizes.

## 15. Motion

Timing:

- Instant feedback: 100–140ms
- Buttons and links: 180–220ms
- Cards and menus: 240–300ms
- Section entrances: 500–700ms
- Hero sequence: 900–1300ms total

Primary easing: `cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)`

- Hero loads in one controlled sequence; content is never delayed unnecessarily.
- Desktop pointer movement may shift the hero field by no more than 4–6px.
- Header becomes slightly opaque and compact after 40–60px of scrolling.
- Product data animations run once using real final values and never loop.
- Animate transform and opacity where possible.
- No scroll hijacking, custom cursor, excessive parallax, or entrance animation on every paragraph.

Reduced motion:

- Remove parallax, chart drawing, counters, and staggered entrances.
- Show final content immediately.
- Preserve focus and essential state feedback.

## 16. Accessibility and Quality

- Semantic landmarks and heading order.
- Keyboard-accessible navigation, menus, and forms.
- Visible focus states using Indigo.
- Escape closes dropdowns and focus returns to the trigger.
- Errors do not rely on color alone.
- Text supports 200% zoom and responsive reflow.
- Body and interface contrast must pass WCAG AA.
- Product visuals require useful alt text or empty alt when decorative.
- Maintain 44px minimum touch targets.
- Test at 320px, 390px, 768px, 1024px, and 1440px.

## 17. Prohibited Patterns

- Generic SaaS template composition
- Inter as the brand font
- Separate hero image under mobile copy
- Floating dashboard cards in the hero
- Device mockups and laptops
- Glassmorphism
- Gradient headline text
- Excessive purple
- Tiny mobile copy
- Repeated equal three-card grids
- Heavy shadows
- Giant radii everywhere
- Fake statistics, testimonials, events, or customers
- Generic AI marketing copy
- Stock student photography
- Newsletter signup
- A visible Home navigation item

## 18. Implementation Boundary

The first implementation milestone includes only:

- Project foundation needed by the homepage
- Public header and navigation
- Homepage
- Footer
- Responsive states
- Motion and reduced-motion behavior
- Necessary structured content/data for these surfaces

Do not invent or fully build Portfolio, Guides, About, Academy, authentication, checkout, dashboard, or case-study pages during this milestone. Links may point to deliberate coming-soon routes or safe placeholders until those pages are specified.
