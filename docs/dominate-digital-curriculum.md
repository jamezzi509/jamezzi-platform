# Dominate Digital — Course Curriculum & Build Reference
### Marketing & AI for Real Businesses | Jamezzi Academy

*(Formerly "Digital Marketing & AI Masterclass" — renamed in the catalog-wide naming refresh.)*

This is the working curriculum reference for Claude Code to build lessons against. Use
alongside the `academy-ui` and `marketing-tool-ui` skills in this repo — this doc defines
**what** to build, the skills define **how** to build it.

---

## Positioning

From beginner to professional. Combines the original 20-module outline with additions from
competitor research and the interactive lesson format already used across the Jamezzi
catalog. No fixed order relative to other courses — stands alone.

---

## Lesson shell (same shape every lesson, content changes)

1. Eyebrow + Title — "Module X · Lesson Y of Z" + lesson title
2. Lede — 1-2 sentence hook framing why this lesson matters
3. 2-4 content sections (§1, §2, §3...) — one idea per section
4. Illustration/diagram slot — at least one per lesson (diagram, or a live tool-canvas
   interaction for the 8 modules below that use one)
5. Mini-exercise — one small "try it now" task
6. Quiz — 3-5 multiple choice questions on core ideas, not trivia
7. Closing block — always all seven, always in this order:
   - 🧠 What you learned — one-sentence recap
   - ⚠️ Common mistake — the mistake beginners make on this topic
   - 💡 Pro tip — one actionable insight beyond the basics
   - ✅ Your assignment — a real task the student does in their own business
   - 📥 Downloadable — worksheet/template/checklist tied to the lesson (name the file)
   - 📋 Checklist — short checklist version, if applicable
   - 🤖 AI prompt of the lesson — one ready-to-use AI prompt tied to the topic

**Passport stamp system:** each lesson passed (quiz complete) earns a stamp in the
student's "Pasaport Aprantisaj" — the course's signature completion motif, tying into the
customer-journey theme and the diaspora audience's relationship with travel/movement. Keep
this language/motif consistent across all 20 modules.

## Additions beyond the original outline (build these in from the start)

- **Case studies / social proof** — get 3-5 real Haitian-owned businesses or beta students
  to go through the course and document results before public launch.
- **Certificate of completion** — awarded after all 20 modules + capstone.
- **Community layer** — WhatsApp or Facebook group for students; likely a bigger
  differentiator than any single lesson given how central WhatsApp is to this audience.
- **Update commitment** — AI/ads modules get refreshed quarterly (state this on the sales
  page); platforms change fast and "always updated" is a strong competitive signal.

## Cross-course dependency

Once this course is fully built, the "Build Your Income Engine" flagship course's Module 8
(the condensed Digital Marketing overview) should be trimmed down to a pointer/link-out to
this course rather than duplicating content — don't build that trim until Dominate Digital
is actually complete.

## Research accuracy standard (applies here same as every Jamezzi course)
No factual inaccuracies. Platform-specific details (ad platform UI, current AI tool
capabilities, monetization thresholds, current API/policy specifics) change fast — verify
current info via web search before writing lesson content that states a specific number,
threshold, or step-by-step flow for a real external platform. Haitian Creole text must be
verified against a dictionary before use, same standard as the rest of the catalog.
